About Teeth Whitening

Professional teeth whitening can lighten your teeth by several shades in just one session. Dubai dental clinics offer in-office treatments using advanced LED or laser technology, as well as custom take-home whitening kits. Professional whitening is safer and more effective than over-the-counter products, with results lasting 6-12 months with proper maintenance.
